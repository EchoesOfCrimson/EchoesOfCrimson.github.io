How you get up there

Back it, and you're
on the brick.

The Backer Wall is a reward tier. Pledge at that level or above and your name goes up in the order your pledge came in — not by how much you gave, not alphabetically. Whoever's early is high on the wall, and nothing moves them.

After the campaign closes we'll send a short survey asking exactly how you want to be named. Real name, handle, the name of someone you've lost — your call. Leave it blank and you stay off the wall entirely; nobody gets listed who didn't ask to be.

Get the launch alert Or read the full campaign.

Names are added by hand, usually within a few days of a pledge landing. If yours isn't up after a week, chase us — it'll be our mistake, not yours.
