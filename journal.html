Prep, the shoot, and two people arguing over the same footage. Newest first.

Day 00July 2026
Pre‑production

Before a single frame

Nothing's been shot yet. We're writing this from the wrong side of it — scripts locked, locations scouted, fake blood ordered in a quantity that got a look at the till. August is when the cameras roll. This is us telling you before, not after.

The experiment: one shoot, one cast, one set of performances in the fog and cobbles of 1888 Whitechapel. Then the footage goes into two rooms and comes out as two films. Eileen cuts the comedy horror — chaos, teeth, jokes that shouldn't land and do. Jason cuts the dark one — slower, colder, the version that follows you home. Neither is the "real" cut. That's the point.

We're making it either way. Our own money's already down and we're not waiting for anyone's blessing. The Kickstarter decides how far it travels, nothing more. If you're going to back it, back it early — early is the only thing that actually moves the needle.
